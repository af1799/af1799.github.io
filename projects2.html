Duke vs. the Gang

Duke vs. the Gang is a game I worked on as a programmer for the 2025–2026 DGA Studios Project. I contributed to UI programming, narrative design, progression backend systems, and dialogue scripting. In addition, I helped develop the dialogue system and authored in-game narrative content, served as programming lead intern contributing to technical direction and implementation, and designed and implemented a player loadout UI along with a progression system to support flexible gameplay configurations. You can view the project on Steam or play it on itch.io!
